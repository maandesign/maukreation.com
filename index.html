About Us

We are a dynamic team of digital experts who bring you solutions

JMD Design is a full-fledged design and development services company based in Mauritius. Established in 2019 to help SMEs and large enterprises achieve a successful business with a unique digital presence. We specialize in digitalization, marketing, graphics and web design.

We craft insights and experiences and provide solutions to ensure the success of your business. We focus on delivering high-quality best-adapted work conveying the right messages to your audience.

We aim to make long-term business interactions between clients and users in the digital world. If you need to start a project and think you need our help, give us a call or write us an email, we'd love to hear from you.

Mission

Our mission is to conceptualize digital solutions and deliver the WOW factor through our services.

Vision

Our vision is to create a digital world full of creativity, meaning and connection for businesses.

Values

Our values are the love, creativity and passion we have for design and attention to every detail.
